SteadyEDGE Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your SteadyEDGE account and associated data, please email us at steadyedgeapp@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my SteadyEDGE account.

Please include the email address connected to your SteadyEDGE account so we can locate the correct account.

What will be deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete account data associated with your SteadyEDGE account, including:

  • Your account profile information
  • Your saved Edges
  • Your logged responses
  • Your training progress
  • Related app data connected to your account

What may be retained

Some information may be retained when required for legal, security, fraud prevention, tax, billing, or compliance purposes. Subscription and purchase history may also remain with the payment provider or app store as required by their policies.

How long deletion takes

We will process account deletion requests within 30 days after receiving the request and confirming the account email.
